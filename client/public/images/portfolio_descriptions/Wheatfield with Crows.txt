I have always enjoyed the Impressionistic, Neo-impressionistic era, Van Gogh being my favorite, Monet, Manet, Seurat there are countless. Regardless, one of my first attempts at oil painting was Starry Night by Van Gogh. It now adorns my brothers walls. Every now and again I simply enjoy painting the masters.
