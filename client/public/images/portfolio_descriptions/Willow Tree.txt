It seems I am always on a pursuit to challenge myself.  Carving in Grey Linoleum proved successful.  It started as one idea and turned into something completely different.  There has always been three phases to the Grey Linoleum Pieces.  Hopefully that phase will come to fruition this year, 2026.  I would elude to it, but would not wish to detract from the current state of this artwork.

A first attempt at this new medium.  My initial premise was to diligently carve the willow tree, then use it as a traditional print block to mass produce prints.  Yet the more I worked on it, the more I focused on texture and depth, the more I realized it would eventually find itself framed and hanging on a wall. 

The idea set me on a path to eventually to create more, with continued emphasis on light, shadow, texture, and detail. 

Material: 
Grey Linoleum on Particle Board

Dimensions:      
18"(w) x 12" (h) x 1.25"(d)
