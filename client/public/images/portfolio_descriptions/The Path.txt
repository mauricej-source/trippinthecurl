This is the back porch view from my first apartment in Orlando, Florida.  It was a small little apartment, but it was next to work and a beautiful body of water.  

Beneath cool afternoon autumn skies, somewhere on the east side of Orlando, Florida a reflective body of water awaits.  Walking down to the shore-line you can hear the patter of geckos, the chirp of frogs, and the ever-so silent alligator skimming along the surface.  

A slender woman, whose attire is both relaxing and comforting.  A t-shirt, cutoff blue-jean shorts, and a large patterned checkered flannel flaps about her hips.  She walks her little dog and simply stops.  Taking the leash off the dog, she lets it go about it's business, as she finds herself seated along the path.  The mirrored surface reflecting every nuance of the cloud cover.  The periodic breeze blowing her flowing blond hair.  She breaths a sigh of relief and lets her shoulders relax.  She has found her moment on the path and it is blissful.
