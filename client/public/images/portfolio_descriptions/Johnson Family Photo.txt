The Family Portrait -- my father actually requested this piece.  He wanted something to hang in the den.  To this day, it continues to hang on the den walls.  What is interesting about this piece, is that we did not sit for this photograph nor does one exist.  This was a series of piecemealed images, starting with my father and working my way from left to right.  At one point i found myself using drivers licenses images to finish the overall likeness of each family member.  Mom's only request was to make her beautiful.  At the time of drawing her hair was not that long.  That was one liberty I took with mom's portrait.
