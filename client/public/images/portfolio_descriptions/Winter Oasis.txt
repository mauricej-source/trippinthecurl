A continued experimentation of mixed mediums to capture both perspective and depth. Continuing with the work performed, on 'Chiseled Barn', the sculpture was cleaned then layer after layer, of oil paint was applied, until the scene was saturated with rich wintry colors to compliment it's depth

Material: 
Oil Paint on Grey Linoleum on Particle Board

Dimensions:      
18"(w) x 12" (h) x 1.25"(d)
