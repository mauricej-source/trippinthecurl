As we approached the 2020 Hurricane Season, I thought I would take a moment and reminisce about the 2019 Hurricane Season.  It provided me the necessary inspiration to bring this particular piece to fruition.

Hurricane Dorian was to decimate Florida from bottom to top.  By the time it reached Jacksonville, Florida it remained approximately 100 miles off the east coast, before proceeding further north. 

Regardless, beautiful pale blue skies, bathed in deep water blues and aquamarine high-lights.  This was a wonderful piece to have had the opportunity to paint.  I hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed painting it.
