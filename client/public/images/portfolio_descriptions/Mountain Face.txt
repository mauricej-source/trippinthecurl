The third attempt and final attempt in this series. Truly has been a journey and quite an experience. This was a new medium to me. One I can definitely see exploring further. Something I had never imagined finding myself working in.

Compensating for texture, detail, depth, and lighting my original inspiration was Half Dome out in the state of California. It turned into a VanGogh'esk Impressionistic, Textured Scene.

I enjoy the variations and hey it looks great on a wall.

I have already started painting the piece, however still appreciate its raw form.

Material:
Grey Linoleum on Particle Board

Dimensions:
18"(w) x 12" (h) x 1.25"(d)
