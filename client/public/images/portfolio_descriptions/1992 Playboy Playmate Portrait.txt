A continued conquest to conquer the elusive portrait.  To draw is one thing, to draw portraits is quite another.  

One ear is lower than the other.  One eye might be higher than the other.  Pupils might not be the same exact size.  The Human Condition comes in all shapes and sizes.  There is no manner of perfection to be had.  

To hone that skill, to draw a portrait can be quite time consuming.   I had made several attempts prior to this one.  Where else was a young man to get beautiful models.  Ones that could be drawn over and over again until perfection, at least the perspective of perfection, was achieved.

The 1992 Playboy Playmate of the Year was probably one of my better attempts at that time in my life.  I do not recall her name, but I do recall that she road Harley Davidson's.  How cool I thought that was and could not wait to ride one of my own some day.
