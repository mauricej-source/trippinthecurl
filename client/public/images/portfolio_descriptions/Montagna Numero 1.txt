Another Bob Ross inspiration.  His palette knife work always fascinated me and having never attempted such a technique, I decided to throw myself into it.   A beautiful snow covered mountain scene that currently hangs amongst so many other oil paintings.  Every-time I look at this piece I want to grab a rowboat and oars and just row to the center and dip my toes in the mirrored waters, of Moraine Lake.

Material: 
Oil on Museum Quality Wrap Canvas with black painted gallery style edges

Dimensions:      
30"(w) x 24" (h) x 1.5"(d)
