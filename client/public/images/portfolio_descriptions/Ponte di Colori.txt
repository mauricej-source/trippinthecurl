If you have not been able to tell, I am originally from the mid-west.  So I grew up with all 4 seasons.  The autumn months have always been my favorite.  It is the one season out of the year, where mother nature gets to be an artist too. 

A brief stroll through one of the many Indiana State Parks, on a cool Autumn afternoon. Only to come upon an old masonic wonder. One that has stood the test of time. Can you envision it.  I know that was my inspiration.
