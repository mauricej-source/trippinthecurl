Another challenging and interesting piece.  This was the combination of a request by my mother, in addition to an attempt at "Pointillism" and a first attempt at using Inks with pencil work.  Needless to say this turned out extremely well and I have also received multiple compliments on it over the years.

I can suggest, this was a first and last attempt at "Pointillism".  I dreamed about this pieces for months afterwards.  Something about patiently placing one pencil mark on a matte-board, over, and over, and over eventually finds its way into your subconscious.

With so many people desiring the original, in addition to my mother, I thought what better way to share the work with everyone, than through the distribution of prints.  Having never done prints, it was a research project of its own.  Purchased my first flat-bed scanner.  One of many technological toys to come.

Overall, there were multiple lessons learned, but because of this piece I later went on to attempt other mixed medium projects.

Material: 
Pencil, Ink on Matte-board

Dimensions:      
20"(w) x 16" (h) x 0.25"(d)
