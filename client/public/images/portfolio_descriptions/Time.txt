Over the course of our planet's fragile state, wind, water, and time have carved, molded and formed something ever so breathtaking. Serenity can be found in natural beauty. What better way to embrace it than to capture it's its meticulous detail onto canvas.

The original intent of this piece was to push myself, to add as much detail as possible.  Most was brushed with a 2.5" fine brush.  Sometimes I am surprised I did not wear it down to the knub.  

To this day I still recall painting this piece, in that small Orlando Apartment, just outside of downtown.  Looking out the window, through my enclosed lanai, onto the large body of reflective water, that would become its own painting.  

This piece was entered into a 2019 1340 Art Magazine, "Call For Artist". It made it to the final round however was not chosen for publication. I have found over the years what wins, tends to be considered ground braking, mixed mediums, contemporary, or outright abstract.

I will continue to push myself.  It would be nice to win a contest sometime within my lifetime.  I would take great pride in that.  That much is certain.
