Over the course of our planet's fragile state, wind, water, and time have carved, molded and formed something ever so breathtaking. Serenity can be found in natural beauty. What better way to embrace it than to capture it's its meticulous detail onto canvas.

The original intent of this piece was to push myself, to add as much detail as possible.  Most was brushed with a 2.5" fine brush.  Sometimes I am surprised I did not wear it down to the knub.  Regardless, this piece was entered into a 2019 1340 Art Magazine, "Call For Artist". It made it to the final round however was not chosen for publication. I look forward to entering another soon.

Material: 
Oil on Museum Quality Wrap Canvas with black painted gallery style edges

Dimensions:      
30"(w) x 30" (h) x 1.5"(d)
