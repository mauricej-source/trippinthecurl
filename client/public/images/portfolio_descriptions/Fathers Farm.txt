I started this piece with a particular idea in mind. As I continued to paint, I found myself beautifully enamored within the detail. The more detail I added the more my mind floated into bliss. I found myself painting trees, pasture, and grass without realizing what I was doing. After a few days it felt as if no sooner had I started, I found myself wondering why my feet hurt, only to find I had been painting for 5 sometimes 6 hours straight. It is a beautiful world to find yourself engulfed by.  I love imagination.
