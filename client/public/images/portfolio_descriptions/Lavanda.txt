The story behind this piece is interesting. This originally started at as an Acrylic Painting. While in Indiana, I attended several "Wine n Canvas" Events.  It was not my first time attending and to be honest most would approach me and tell me I was cheating or I did not belong in such classes, because of the quality of work. I would go anyway, because they were fun for me and helped me practice my paint times.  I also enjoyed the pure pleasure of others embracing the wonderful world of art.

A Tuscan Landscape, a gentle breeze, a faint smell of lavender in the air. It represents all the soothing aspects of the senses.  I enjoyed the scene so, that I eventually brought it home.  Placed it upon my easel and repainted so of the areas, adding additional color, depth, and definition.  

Material: 
Acrylic & Oil on Museum Quality Wrap Canvas with white gallery style edges

Dimensions:  20"(w) x 16" (h) x 0.50"(d)
