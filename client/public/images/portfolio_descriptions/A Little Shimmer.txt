Shortly after finding the many great works of Bob Ross, I found myself compelled to try his repeated "Wet on Wet" Technique.  Cloud cover has always been somewhat of a painstaking process in the past.  No more.  This piece represents a continued practice of capturing everything beautiful about nature.  A fisherman's dream, after a long day, the sun finally setting upon the horizon, as the days work is tallied, and the distant call of the dinner bell rings.

Material:
Oil on Museum Quality Wrap Canvas with black painted gallery style edges

Dimensions:      
18"(w) x 36" (h) x 0.75"(d)
