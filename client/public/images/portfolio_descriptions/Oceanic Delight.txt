I started this project July of 2021. I was making significant progress and then I hit the proverbial imaginative brick wall. The inspiration for this piece was the result of an exchange of ideas with a Jacksonville friend of mine.  

It is currently a that is a work in progress.  There will come a day, where I will eventually dust off the cobwebs and finish this watery wrestling match.  

Oil on museum quality canvas, 40 in x 30 in x 2 in.
