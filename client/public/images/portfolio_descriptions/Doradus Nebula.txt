This is a piece that I finished a very long time ago, early 2005ish if I recall.  It represents a time in my life where I felt lost, angry at the world, uncertain of what my path was or how I should even find it. Maybe that is simply the plight of a young man. In taking on this project, I wanted to test the level of detail I could achieve with each brush stroke.  Needless to say I wore out the tips of several. 

I consider this a Contemporary Piece because I have painted it across 2 separate 16x20" Museum Quality Canvases.  I also painted the edges of the canvases as if the nebula had engulfed them.  To post it here, I have merely stitched them together.

Some key words that might describe that point in my life; chaos, nebulous, space, twists, turns, unknown, uncertainty, slightly askew even

Material:  Oil on Museum Quality Canvas with the sides painted

Dimensions:  2 by (16"(w) x 20" (h) x 0.5"(d))
