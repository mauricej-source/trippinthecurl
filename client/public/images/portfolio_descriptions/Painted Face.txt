A piece I have been working on for quite some time. It finally found its way to fruition. It represents the third in a series of wall sculptures. Intricately carved only to then be painted. Truly has been a journey and quite an experience. This was a new medium to me. One I can definitely see exploring further. Something I had never imagined finding myself working in. I have some new ideas that are larger in scale. Just need to follow up with some additional research, design, and of course purchase new supplies. Sometimes its the best moment in create art, conceptualization.

Compensating for texture, detail, depth, and lighting my original inspiration was Half Dome out in the state of California. It turned into a VanGogh'esk Impressionistic, Textured Scene.

I enjoy the variations and hey it looks great on a wall.

Material:
Grey Linoleum on Particle Board

Dimensions:
18"(w) x 12" (h) x 1.25"(d)
