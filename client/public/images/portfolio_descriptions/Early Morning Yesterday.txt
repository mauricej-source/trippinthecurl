Finally put the finishing touches on this piece.  This particular piece is the start of a new journey.  It is a first of many attempts at trying to find my own style.  My own painting technique.  It also is the first Acrylic I have done in quite some time.  Definitely makes you think or respond to the canvas quicker.  My inspiration for this piece was actually New Years Day, 2023.  I had stayed in Saint Augustine overnight.  It was an extremely foggy January 1st.  Waking early and packing the truck, I started the short trek home back to Jacksonville.  It occurred to me that I wanted some good coffee so I headed into downtown Saint Augustine.  Crossing the Vilano Beach Bridge, the sun started to crest the horizon.  It was something I just could not resist capturing.  From that day forward I knew this would become a painting.

Material: 
Acrylic on Museum Quality Wrap Canvas with black painted gallery style edges

Dimensions:      
24"(w) x 36" (h) x 1.5"(d)
