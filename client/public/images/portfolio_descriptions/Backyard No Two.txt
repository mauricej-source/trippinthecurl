This Autumn Scene was a Commissioned Request made by my older cousins, on my mother's side of the family.  It was an idea and wish to be a Christmas Gift for their parents.  As we were having the conversation, I recall asking 'Are you sure?'  The question came shortly after the picture the presented for me to paint.   'Oh, yes, yes, that view, of the yard, is my father's favorite part of the house.  He always enjoys mowing that part of the yard.  He also enjoys the Autumn Colors each and every year.  They referred to it as their parent's inner serenity.

So I set out to paint the Autumn scene.  Starting in the background, slowly working my way to the foreground.  I was deliberately meticulous not only about the Autumn Colors, but the placement of them.  To paint someone's idea of serenity, was a little daunting to say the least.  I not only wanted it to pop, but I wanted it to portray and have the same manner of feelings as if they were truly in their own backyard.

It has been hanging on their wall since 2016.  My hope is they have enjoyed it as much as I have enjoyed painting it.
