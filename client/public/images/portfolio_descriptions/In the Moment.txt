This piece represents an attempt at forcing myself to think outside of my comfort zone.

Different techniques were chosen for cloud cover, background, mid-ground, and foreground.  In addition, both contrasting colors and mid-ground were chosen as areas of focus.

The lesson I took away is that we must continuously strive to be a better person than the moment before.

Also worth noting, this is the first piece I have painted someone into the scenery. 

Material: 
Oil on Museum Quality Wrap Canvas with white gallery style edges

Dimensions:      
24"(w) x 18" (h) x 0.75"(d)
