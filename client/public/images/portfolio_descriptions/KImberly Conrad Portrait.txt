What better way to perfect my drawings skills by sketching Hugh Heffner's Wife.  Well one of many wives.  This particular one makes me smile, because it took countless erasures to finally produce a portrait I was proud of.  The piece also mimics another artist's technique, one that he was very well known for, Patrick Nagel.  It is interesting, as a young man I can remember how great I thought Playboy was.  Not only did it have everything a young man desired, but the artwork was phenomenal, the playmates were my models, and it even had the occasional article that was worth a read.  Nagel proved to be one of my influences over the course of my life, as an artist.
